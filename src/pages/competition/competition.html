What’s included in the subscription ?

Registered participants have the right to :

  • A Beta Steam key for the latest version of the game RETIMED
  • Free participation to every Minor and Major, within the limits of the available slots.
  • Priority over punctual participants when registering for Minors and Majors.
  • Integration of the player’s profile in the global ranking
  • Invitation to the Grand Final for the best of them.

Registered participants do not have the right to:

  • A free entrance to the event hosting the Minors and the Majors. They are required to pay the costs relative to the events hosting the Minors and the Majors (Visitor entry fee)
  • Systematic technical support in the event of a set-up error. Support is available within the availability of the studio only.
